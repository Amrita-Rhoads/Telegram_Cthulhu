A new game of Cthulhu has been created for this chat and is currently pending. 

/joingame [nickname] - joins the game under an optional nickname.
/unjoin - unjoins you from a pending game.
/listplayers - lists pending players.
/spectate - spectate this game.
/unspectate - removes you as a spectator.
/endgame - cancels this pending game.
/startgame - starts the pending game.

If you join the game, you must either message @cthulhu_game_bot or have messaged @cthulhu_game_bot in the past -- otherwise, you will not receive your role or hand!

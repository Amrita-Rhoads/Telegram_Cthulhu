You've disabled forced claims, which means that using the /claim command is optional and players can update their claims at any time. To force players to claim in order before investigation can start, use the command "/claimsettings on".

How to claim:
/claim 2 - claims 2 elder signs
/claim C - claims 1 cthulhu and 0 elder signs.
/claim 1 1 - claims 1 elder sign and 1 cthulhu.
/claim 2 C - claims 2 elder signs and 1 cthulhu.

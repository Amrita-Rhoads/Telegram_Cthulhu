You've enabled forced claims, which means that players must claim in order before anyone can investigate or change their claim. To disable this, use the command "/claimsettings off".

How to claim:
/claim 2 - claims 2 elder signs
/claim C - claims 1 cthulhu and 0 elder signs.
/claim 1 1 - claims 1 elder sign and 1 cthulhu.
/claim 2 C - claims 2 elder signs and 1 cthulhu.
